## 6. 5 new Pattern candidates at v21

### #36 Pseudonymous Researcher Archetype

**Formal statement:**
> Intentionally anonymous authorship distinct from solo-known / corporate / LLC / community-viral archetypes. Motivations: legal-risk mitigation, employment, geographic, harassment avoidance, security research community norm.

**Evidence:** 1 (x1xhlol).
**Required:** 2+ pseudonymous framework authors.
**Prediction:** May emerge more as controversial-content aggregators multiply.

### #37 Crypto-Donation-Funded Scale Path

**Formal statement:**
> Multi-channel donations (Patreon + Ko-fi + crypto + project token) as scale-path revenue without institutional funding. Works at 100K+ scale when community-amplification + zeitgeist align.

**Evidence:** 1 (system-prompts-leaks at 135K with 6 channels + Solana token).
**Required:** 2+ similar monetization structures.
**Prediction:** Alternative scale-path increasingly visible as AI OSS experiments with tokens.

### #38 Prompt-Leak-Archive Genre

**Formal statement:**
> 3rd outside-scope sub-type after education-aggregator + foundation-model. Extracts from closed-source commercial tools; gray-zone legal position; pseudonymous-author common.

**Evidence:** 1 (system-prompts-leaks).
**Required:** 2+ similar archives.
**Prediction:** May remain rare due to legal-complexity; related genres (leaked training data, benchmark data) may emerge.

### #39 Controversial-License-Use

**Formal statement:**
> OSS licenses applied to content arguably-uncopyrightable or not-owned-by-licensor. Social signal rather than enforceable legal claim.

**Evidence:** 1 (system-prompts-leaks GPL-3.0 on extracted prompts).
**Required:** 2+ similar applications.
**Prediction:** Will appear more as aggregator/archive genres multiply.

### #40 Derivative-Security-Service Perverse-Incentive

**Formal statement:**
> Repo author operates commercial security service monetizing from concerns the OSS repo helps create. Distinct from responsible-security-research norms (coordinated disclosure).

**Evidence:** 1 (system-prompts-leaks → ZeroLeaks by x1xhlol).
**Required:** 2+ similar structures.
**Prediction:** May remain rare but structurally significant.
