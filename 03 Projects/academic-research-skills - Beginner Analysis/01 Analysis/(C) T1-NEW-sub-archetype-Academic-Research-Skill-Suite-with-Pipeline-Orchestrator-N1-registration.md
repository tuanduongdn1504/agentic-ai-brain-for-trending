Considered Library-vocab vehicle (#7 in v2.3 §3 catalog) for "Nested-Versioned-Sub-Skill Architecture with Pipeline-Orchestrator". REJECTED because:

1. **Structural-typing semantics**: T1 sub-archetype membership is a structural-type property (the subject IS a member of the sub-archetype, with all its 5 structural properties). Library-vocab is a vocabulary item (the subject HAS the property, possibly partially). Losing the IS-A semantics loses the corpus-classification value.

2. **Multi-property bundling**: 5 load-bearing properties cohere as a single structural identity. Splitting them into 5 separate Library-vocab items (Nested-Versioned-Sub-Skill + Pipeline-Orchestrator + Academic-Research-Vertical + 42-Agent-Scale + Material-Passport-Audit-Layer) loses the conjunction-membership semantics.

3. **Corpus-classification utility**: Future T1 subjects can be evaluated against this sub-archetype as a single classification axis. Library-vocab items are evaluated independently with weaker classification utility.

4. **Precedent**: v81 taste-skill, v83 open-design, v84 image-blaster, v74 LLMs-from-scratch all chose sub-archetype vehicle over Library-vocab when 3+ structural properties cohered. Consistent with established Phase 4b vehicle-selection discipline.
