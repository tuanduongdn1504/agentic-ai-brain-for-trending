## 9. Why lineage acknowledgment matters

### Corpus value

Explicit lineage acknowledgment:
- Contextualizes framework contribution (what's novel vs inherited)
- Credits prior work (community norm)
- Signals intellectual honesty
- Helps researchers trace ideas

### Lineage amnesia risk

Frameworks without lineage acknowledgment:
- May appear more novel than they are
- Community attribution lost over time
- Patterns re-invented without history

### Pattern observation

**Research-paper-chain (archetype 4) always cites.** Individual-author (archetype 1) frequently cites. Methodology (archetype 2) cites methodologies. Community-viral subdivides:
- **3a**: Don't cite (may have amnesia or genuine novelty)
- **3b**: Cite community-viral predecessors (meta-community-viral)

Skyvern's 3b pattern is **honest community-viral framework**.
