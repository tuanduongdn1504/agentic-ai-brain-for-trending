## What is being proposed

Register a NEW **Library-vocabulary item** capturing the methodology pattern observed at v87:

> **"Token-Economy-Quantification per Communication Style"** — a design discipline where each prompt-style in a multi-style product is assigned an explicit % output-token-impact rating (e.g. "65-75% fewer", "+20-40% more") with a 3-tier visual badge taxonomy (🟢 fewer / 🟡 slightly fewer / 🔴 more) so the cost-axis becomes the **primary discriminator** for style selection, equal-or-prior to aesthetic / use-case axes.

## Why this matters

Prior corpus subjects quantified token-economy at **aggregate** or **architectural** scope:

| Wiki | Quantification scope |
|---|---|
| v76 agent-skills-standard | "85% fewer tokens" — aggregate across 259-skill bundle; routing-architecture-attributable |

**v87 is the first corpus subject to quantify token-economy at PER-STYLE / PER-FEATURE granularity as a first-class design axis.** Each of the 13 styles carries its own explicit % range AND a categorical badge. The cost-axis is given equal-or-prior weight to the use-case axis in the README's discriminator tables.

This is a methodology-vocabulary item worth tracking, not a structural Pattern. Hence Library-vocab registration rather than Pattern promotion.
