v8 extracted architecture lessons explicitly. But this risks EVERY non-agent wiki being "meta-reference." 

**Action:** Routine v2 — meta-reference only when wiki has legitimate architecture/governance lessons (not every outside-scope wiki).

### 5. CodeCrafters business model analysis lightly treated
