Initial probe found `CLAUDE.md` at repo root for codegraph — corpus-rare. Most corpus subjects don't ship CLAUDE.md at root level (they ship AGENTS.md or platform-specific configs).

**Lesson captured:** OC candidate "Rule-Density-As-AI-Optimization" (terse + rule-dense governance files for AI agent consumption). Could differentiate corpus subjects.

**Lesson captured:** OC candidate "Multi-Platform Config-Target Maintenance Cost" — cost grows linearly with platform-count. Routine v2.3 codification candidate: when wiki-building corpus subjects that span multiple platforms, expect non-trivial maintenance evidence in CHANGELOG.
