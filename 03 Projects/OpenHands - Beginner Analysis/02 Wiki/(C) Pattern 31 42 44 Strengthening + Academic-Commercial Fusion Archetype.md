## 2. Pattern #31 Open-Core Commercial Entity — N=3 strengthening

### Confirmed pattern formal statement (unchanged from v24)

> **Pattern #31 Open-Core Commercial Entity (CONFIRMED v24):** Organizational archetype for frameworks where commercial entity founds both OSS product + proprietary commercial tier simultaneously (not community-to-commercial transition). OSS core uses restrictive or standard license protecting against vendor appropriation while preserving community access. Proprietary tier adds domain-specific differentiator unavailable in OSS.

### N=3 data point evidence

| # | Wiki | Scope sub-type | OSS license | Proprietary differentiator | Entity | Commercial tier |
|---|------|---------------|-------------|---------------------------|--------|-----------------|
| 1 | v20 fish-speech | Outside-scope foundation-model | Custom non-OSI ("Fish Audio Research License") | Anti-distillation + attribution clauses | 39 AI, INC. | Paid commercial license |
| 2 | v24 Skyvern | T5 browser-agent | AGPL-3.0 single | Proprietary anti-bot on Cloud | Skyvern-AI | Skyvern Cloud managed |
| 3 | **v30 OpenHands** | **T5 SWE-agent** | **MIT core + separate-license enterprise directory** | **Kubernetes + RBAC + Slack/Jira/Linear integrations** | **All Hands AI** | **Free Cloud tier + paid Enterprise tier** |

### Sub-type + license variant diversity

- **3 scope sub-types:** foundation-model / browser-agent / SWE-agent
- **3 license variants:** custom non-OSI / AGPL-3.0 / MIT+separate-enterprise
- **3 commercial differentiators:** license-exclusion / proprietary-cloud-feature / enterprise-integration-bundle
- **3 entity types:** Inc with research license / Commercial-AI with Cloud / Commercial-AI with enterprise+academic-founders

**Pattern #31 now validated across 3 orthogonal axes** (scope / license / differentiator) — pattern is robust, not artifact of single sub-type.

## 3. Pattern #32 Research-Paper-Chain Lineage — N=3 strengthening

> **Pattern #32 Research-Paper-Chain Lineage (CONFIRMED v22):** Framework architecture grounded in explicit chain of prior research publications. Multi-paper chain where follow-on papers cite and extend prior work. Distinct from single-paper citation or community-viral lineage.

### N=3 data point evidence

|---|------|-------------|

### Chain-strength meta-observation

v30 OpenHands is **first corpus data point with 2 papers authored by overlapping team within same framework family.** Prior chains either:
- Single paper + references to unrelated prior work (LlamaFactory cites PEFT/QLoRA but authors don't overlap)
- Single paper + no follow-up (multica / paperclip / Skyvern)

**OpenHands chain strength = highest in corpus** (4 persistent authors across 16-month gap between papers). Evidence of stable research program, not one-off publication.
