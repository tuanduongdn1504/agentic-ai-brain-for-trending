### Pattern #9 refinement (formal statement at v15)

**v10-v13 version:** "Tiers bifurcate along corporate-vs-solo axis."

**v14 challenge:** paperclip doesn't fit clean bifurcation.

**v15 new hypothesis:** "**Pattern #9 is tier-dependent:**
- **Platform tiers (T2)** — HOMOGENEOUSLY community-platform (economics favor community contribution over corporate/solo)
- **Bridge tiers (T4)** — BIFURCATE corporate-vs-solo cleanly (owned-service drives official vs unofficial split)
- **Application tiers (T5)** — CAN bifurcate but admits community-platform (autonomy + integration complexity favors teams)
- **Assistant tiers (T1)** — WIDEST diversity (6 archetypes at N=6 including VN-authored-solo + LLC + solo + YC-corporate + community-platform + others)"

### Why this refinement makes sense economically

- **T2 platforms** require sustained infrastructure investment — difficult for solo, unnecessary for corporate (no strategic advantage). Sweet spot = community-platform with distributed contribution.
- **T4 bridges** have natural official-vs-unofficial split because bridges connect to owned services. Official = publisher. Unofficial = community reverse-engineer.
- **T5 applications** have high autonomy complexity — solos manage (Karpathy narrow) OR corporations fund (ByteDance broad) OR community-platforms coordinate (paperclip hybrid).
- **T1 assistants** have lowest barrier — solos and corporations and communities all produce them easily.

## 8. Edges + failure modes (of analysis)

### Pattern #9 refinement risks

- **Ad-hoc subcategorization** — adding tier-dependence may be rationalizing away counter-evidence
- **Small sample** — T2 N=2 is minimum validation; 3rd entrant could contradict
- **Bias toward rich taxonomy** — analyst preference vs parsimony
- **Category overlap** — community-platform criteria partial fit (goclaw 6.5/7) shows fuzzy boundaries

### Community-platform archetype concerns

- **Definitional** — 7-criteria may be too permissive (most non-solo non-corporate fit)
- **Missing archetype** — other T1/T4/T5 entrants might fit community-platform on closer look (retrospective analysis Q)
- **Not empirically distinct** — if "no corporate + no solo" is defining, it's residual category

### Storm Bear corpus maturity

14-15 wikis + Pattern #9 tested at 3 tiers = sufficient for predictive use. But:
- Sample size still small
- Pattern tests are hypothesis-testing not theorem-proving
- Honest uncertainty preserved in resolutions (not forced clean)
