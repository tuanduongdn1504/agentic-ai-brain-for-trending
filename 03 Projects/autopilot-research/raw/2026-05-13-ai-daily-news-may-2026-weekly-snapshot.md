sleep [1]. However, they do not address **error handling or intervention 
strategies** for when an agent makes a high-stakes mistake, such as

tax" where extra tokens are injected into requests [3], and Sisinty notes that

like "humanity's last exam" [4] or subjective "vibes" [6, 7]. Production systems
require **rigorous internal evaluation frameworks**—systematic unit tests for 
prompts to ensure that a model update (like the rumored "nerfing" of Claude 4.6)

*   **Compliance and Data Governance:** Sisinty warns about security breaches at
Vercel [10] and privacy concerns with OpenAI's "Chronicle" [11]. However, there 
is no mention of **GDPR, HIPAA, or SOC2 compliance**. For a production

*   **Latency and Performance SLAs:** The sources focus on what is possible

through coordinates using Python before generating an image may be too slow to 
meet production Service Level Agreements (SLAs) [12, 13].
