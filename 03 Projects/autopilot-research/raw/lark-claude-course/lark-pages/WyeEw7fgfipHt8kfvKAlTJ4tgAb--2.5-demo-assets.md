🎨 Brand Voice Pack A — "Playful + Data-driven"​
Context: cho sản phẩm tiêu dùng/văn phòng, tone Anglo-Saxon​
​

Example 1 — Coffee maker:​
"Brews 12 cups in 8 min flat. Built for offices that run ​
on espresso and inside jokes."​
​
Example 2 — Standing desk:​
"Adjusts in 4 sec, supports 80kg. Your back will write a ​
thank-you note."​
​
Example 3 — Wireless keyboard:​
"Typing speed +18% vs average mech. Bluetooth pairs in 2 ​
sec — magic, but engineered."​
​
PATTERN:​
- Stat + personality + short (20-30 words)​
- Dry humor, specific, not generic​
- Confident + playful, not try-hard​
​
Task paired: "Viết description cho mouse pad XL gaming"​
Expected output style:​
​
"Tracks 8000 DPI on 90cm x 40cm surface. Your mouse finally has room to breathe — and so does your desk aesthetic."​
