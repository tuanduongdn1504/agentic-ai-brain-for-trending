3.
Create Scheduled Task — automation from workflow đã identify​
4.
Apply Diligence D4 — Creation + Transparency + Deployment​
5.
Ship 1-page Automation & Diligence Plan — living document​
6.
Earn Certificate "AI Fluency Certified" nếu ship đủ 15 outcomes (3 × 5 buổi)​
