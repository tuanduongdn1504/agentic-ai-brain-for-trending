### Registration

> **Pattern #51 Vibe-Coding/Vibe-Design Philosophy Spectrum (CANDIDATE N=2):** Frameworks + content aggregators explicitly position on vibe-coding / vibe-design philosophy spectrum. Anti-vibe pole emphasizes predictability + formal specification + compile-time gates + deterministic outcomes. Pro-vibe pole emphasizes aesthetic-first + descriptive language + creative AI interpretation + "good taste" primacy. Observable corpus trend as "vibe-coding" becomes cultural meme in AI development. Hybrid positions possible (awesome-design-md is 87% spec / 13% vibe per 9-section structure).

### Related to Pattern #13 Autonomy-Framing Spectrum

- #13 = autonomy axis (human-in-loop vs human-out)
- #51 = vibe axis (deterministic spec vs creative vibe)

May generalize as meta-pattern at audit.

## 8. Pattern #50 candidate — Commercial-Funnel Companion Platform

### Distinction from Pattern #31 Open-Core

| **#50 Commercial-Funnel Companion (new)** | OSS content + SEPARATE commercial platform | awesome-design-md (MIT) + getdesign.md (sponsorship) |

### Registration

> **Pattern #50 Commercial-Funnel Companion Platform (CANDIDATE N=1):** Content aggregators or OSS projects use commercial companion platform (separate website with sponsorship / paid features / premium listings) as monetization channel. Distinct from Pattern #31 open-core (proprietary tier on same product). Commercial platform typically hosts preview / catalog / showcase + monetized slots. Cross-drives attention: OSS virality → platform traffic → commercial revenue.
