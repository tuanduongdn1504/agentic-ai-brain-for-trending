## 8. Velocity interpretation

### What 3,000 stars/day means

- ~125 stars/hour
- ~2 stars/minute
- GitHub trending throughout the 20-day period
- Twitter/HN/Reddit sustained amplification
- Viral threshold maintained — not a spike-and-die

### Historical context

Fastest viral OSS projects historically:
- **AutoGPT** (March 2023): ~10K stars/day at peak (~175K in weeks)
- **GPT-Engineer** (mid-2023): ~3-5K stars/day at peak
- **Llama leak** (March 2023): Similar
- **ChatGPT-related repos (2023)**: Multiple peaked at ~1-5K stars/day

awesome-design-md 3K stars/day = **historically significant virality tier** — not AutoGPT-peak but well above typical.

### Sustainability question

Can awesome-design-md sustain 3K stars/day beyond 20 days? Unknown. Historical viral-aggregators often plateau or decline after initial burst. Monitor for:
- Sustained velocity after day 30
- Fork growth (indicates active use, not just collection)
- Discord community growth
- Derivative projects / forks contributing
