## 7. Instructor-centric curriculum implications

### Advantages of named-instructor model

1. **Voice continuity** — instructors can cross-reference their own work across units
2. **Specialization** — instructor focused on area of expertise
3. **Community engagement** — students connect with individuals
4. **Career value** — instructor visibility beyond course

### Potential limitations

1. **Bus factor** — if instructor leaves, course continuity risk
2. **Update pace** — individual instructor availability limits iteration
3. **Bias** — individual perspective may narrow content
4. **Scale** — fewer instructors = limited content throughput

### Microsoft v6 model

Microsoft v6 anonymous-team:
- Corporate-voice continuity
- Easy to scale contributors
- Consistent tone across lessons
- Less individual engagement
