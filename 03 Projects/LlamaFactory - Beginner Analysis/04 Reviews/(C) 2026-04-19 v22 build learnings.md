## 4. What didn't work / friction

### Minor friction

- **Peer-reviewed venue details** — ACL 2024 paper type (long paper vs system demo) not readily fetchable; inferred from context
- **Lab4AI specifics** — academic affiliation details (institution, country, lab lead) not fully documented in README; inferred
- **Optimizer paper references** — GaLore/BAdam/APOLLO/Adam-mini/Muon individual papers not fetched (out of scope)
- **Megatron-core depth** — mentioned but not deeply explored (out of wiki scope)
- **Hyperparameter complexity** — framework simplifies setup but training itself remains hard; beginner guide emphasizes

### No ethical-framing required

Unlike v20 (license) and v21 (content-provenance), LlamaFactory has clean legal + clean ethical position. Beginner guide didn't need ⚠️ "Read this first" section.

**Signal:** not all outside-scope wikis require ethical framing. v2.1 protocol should condition ethical-framing on content-provenance + license-validity + brand-association triggers (not blanket outside-scope).
