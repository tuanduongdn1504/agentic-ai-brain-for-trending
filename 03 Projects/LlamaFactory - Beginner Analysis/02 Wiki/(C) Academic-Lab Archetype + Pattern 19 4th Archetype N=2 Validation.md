## 10. Academic-lab archetype implications

### Continuity advantage

Academic labs have institutional continuity unlike pure-solo:
- Students rotate, but lab continues
- Faculty provide long-term direction
- Grants fund multi-year projects
- Paper-to-paper research trajectory

### Research-velocity advantage

Academic labs can integrate research quickly because:
- Co-author network across institutions
- Pre-publication sharing culture
- Close monitoring of new research (researchers read arXiv daily)
- Students implement new methods as projects

### Trade-offs

- **Commercial constraints:** academic affiliations may limit commercial activities
- **Resource volatility:** grant funding fluctuates
- **Maintenance pressure:** production-quality requires non-research labor
