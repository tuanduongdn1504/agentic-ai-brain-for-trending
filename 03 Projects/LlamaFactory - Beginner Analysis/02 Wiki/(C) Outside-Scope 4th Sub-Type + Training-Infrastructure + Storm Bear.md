**Formal statement:**
> Peer-reviewed publication at academic venue (ACL / NeurIPS / ICLR / ICML / EMNLP) distinct from arXiv-only preprint. Peer-review implies community validation + competitive acceptance. Stronger research signal than preprint-only publication.

**Evidence at v22:** 1 (LlamaFactory ACL 2024).
**Required:** 2+ peer-reviewed frameworks.
**Prediction:** Medium — academic-lab-affiliated frameworks more likely to reach peer-review.

**Formal statement:**

**Evidence at v22:** 1 (LlamaFactory — 5 novel 2024 optimizers + 5 RL methods within research-current window).
**Required:** 2+ research-velocity frameworks.
**Prediction:** Strong — Unsloth, Axolotl, TRL all show similar velocity patterns.
